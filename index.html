BIOGRAPHY

Welcome, I am currently a graduate student in Engineering Physics, specializing in Biomedical Engineering. My main research focus is on AI and Data Science for Health, specifically in the areas of Biomedical Signal Processing (EEG) and Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI). I also participate in interdisciplinary collaborative research projects in Biomechanics field, such as the application of hyperelastics models to the human body and the propagation of acoustics waves in biomedical applications.

Besides my scientific research, I also assist in teaching lower-level courses. I enjoy reading, listening to music, and learning new things.

Objective: To seek PhD opportunities in advanced countries around the world and participate in collaborative scientific research with them.
